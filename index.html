Our Origin

Our Story: From Memory to Masterpiece

Keychain Studio began as a hobby when I was thinking of a perfect gift for my partner. When I was given a beautiful bouquet of flowers, I realized I needed a way to preserve that feeling forever.

"It wasn't just a bouquet; it was a memory I couldn't bear to let fade."

That passion turned into a business idea: Why should finding a special gift be hard? I discovered resin—the most effective and "life-saving" way to preserve cherished items. We make it easy to own a memory instead of waiting days at a store.

Life-Saving Preservation

Freeze time with high-grade resin that captures every petal and detail.

Gifting Made Easy
